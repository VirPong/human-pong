VirPong Created

Our fearless leader, Dr. Sawin collected twenty-one talented individuals from the University of Puget Sound converge their skills to produce a revolutionary Ping-Pong Game. The game will be called VirPong and will allow game users to run around with a Wii Remote while virtually playing the game on their personal cell phone. The current gaming system will only be available for the IPhone and Android systems. Viewing will be available on the website as well! We look forward to a fantastic relationship and may your VirPong experiences begin!

Private Viewing

VirPong was released for a private viewing on the campus of the University of Puget Sound. The game received many positive reviews. A public viewing of the game will be announced shortly.

World Premiere

The World Premiere of VirPong will be hosted at the University of Puget Sound on Wednesday, December 14, 2011 at 4 pm. For directions please use Google Maps.
